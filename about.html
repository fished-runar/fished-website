Our journey

Our Story

Milestone

2018

The founders of Fished, Steffen Pauschert and Even Ovnerud, had an idea back in 2018 to make seafood trading more efficient and reduce the number of manual operations involved in buying and selling seafood products. Their observations of the market led them to notice that some outdated solutions were setting the standards for an entire industry.

Milestone

2020

Fished AS was established as a company, and the foundation for today's Fished Order Management was laid.

Milestone

2022

Fished expanded and hired senior profiles, both experienced profiles with a unique experience and solid track record from building software businesses within the seafood industry.

Milestone

2023

The first two customer contracts were signed, and the final strategy to build a completely new solution for the seafood industry was decided. Fished is hiring additional resources with extensive seafood and technology experience.

Milestone

2024

First customer goes live on the solution, fully integrated with accounting system, production system, customs clearance system and WMS with picking and scanning. Fished hires three new key roles — a new CEO, CTO and front-end developer.

Our approach

By the Industry,
For the Industry

Without technical heritage, Fished is developed from the ground up to meet the specific needs of the seafood industry. With the vision “by the industry — for the industry”, we work closely with our customers to continuously adapt the system to new challenges and opportunities.

Our roadmap is shaped in partnership with selected seafood companies, ensuring that every feature we build solves real problems for real people in the industry.
