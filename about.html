Our Story

Our approach

By the Industry,
For the Industry

Without technical heritage, Fished is developed from the ground up to meet the specific needs of the seafood industry. With the vision “by the industry — for the industry”, we work closely with our customers to continuously adapt the system to new challenges and opportunities.

Our roadmap is shaped in partnership with selected seafood companies, ensuring that every feature we build solves real problems for real people in the industry.
