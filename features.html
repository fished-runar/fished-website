Platform Capabilities

Feature Overview

Built in close collaboration with the seafood industry, every module in Fished is designed around real workflows.

From purchase to delivery,
in one flow

Handle purchase and sales orders with certifications, catch certificates and export documentation — all managed in a single, connected workflow.

  • Purchase & sales orders in one platform
  • Catch certificates and export documentation
  • Real-time status across the supply chain

Real-time control
on the floor

Inventory control built for terminals and floor staff, directly connected to orders and invoicing. No double-entry, no blind spots.

  • Live inventory across all locations
  • Mobile-ready for terminal and floor staff
  • Linked to orders and outbound invoices

Data that drives
better decisions

Live data on sales, margins, volume and costs. Drill down by customer, category or period and export directly to your tools of choice.

  • Sales and margin dashboards
  • Filter by customer, category or date
  • Export to Excel, PDF and integrations

Invoices matched
automatically

Fished reads, interprets and matches inbound invoices to your purchase orders automatically. You approve — we handle the rest.

  • Automatic invoice reading and matching
  • Exception flagging for manual review
  • Full audit trail per invoice

From vessel to customer,
fully traceable

Full traceability across every transaction. Search by batch, product or order and pinpoint origin information in seconds — ready for audits or recalls.

  • End-to-end batch traceability
  • Search by product, vessel or date
  • Audit and recall ready

Your rules,
running automatically

Build workflow rules without writing a line of code. Trigger approvals, notifications and system actions on any platform event.

  • Visual rule builder, no code required
  • Trigger on any platform event
  • Notifications, approvals and system actions

Customer relations,
tied to every order

Track customer history, quotes and activity — connected directly to your orders and invoices. Build lasting relationships backed by real data.

  • Customer activity and history timeline
  • Quotes linked directly to orders
  • Connected to invoicing and documents
