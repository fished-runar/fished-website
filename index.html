What we do

Built for the Seafood Industry,
From the Ground Up

Fished is a cloud-based platform that connects purchase orders, warehouse management, invoicing and integrations in one place — built in close collaboration with the industry, for the industry.

Our approach

Customer-Driven Innovation

Fished is developed in close collaboration with our users, regardless of whether they are large or small players in the seafood industry. Through our built-in feedback function, customers can easily influence future functionality. Our roadmap for further development is shaped in partnership with selected seafood companies, ensuring solutions that meet the real needs of the industry.
